[ISHA-1]
Text: All this whatsoever moves in the universe is pervaded by the Lord. Enjoy through renunciation. Do not covet anyone’s wealth.

[ISHA-2]
Text: One may desire to live a hundred years if one performs one’s duties. Thus action does not bind the one who performs them without attachment.

[ISHA-4]
Text: The Self is unmoving, yet swifter than the mind. The senses cannot reach it, for it ever goes before them.

[ISHA-5]
Text: It moves, and it moves not. It is far, and yet it is near. It is within all this, and yet it is outside all this.

[ISHA-6]
Text: A wise man sees all beings in his own Self and his own Self in all beings. Then he does not hate or reject anyone.

[ISHA-7]
Text: When to the knower all things become one with his own Self, then what delusion, what sorrow can there be?
