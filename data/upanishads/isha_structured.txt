---
REF: ISHA 1
SANSKRIT:
ईशावास्यमिदं सर्वं यत्किञ्च जगत्यां जगत् ।
MEANING:
All this—whatever exists in this universe—is pervaded by the Supreme.
TAGS:
self, universe, knowledge
---

---
REF: ISHA 2
SANSKRIT:
कुर्वन्नेवेह कर्माणि जिजीविषेच्छतं समाः ।
MEANING:
One should desire to live a hundred years by performing actions in a spirit of renunciation.
TAGS:
karma, duty, renunciation
---

---
REF: ISHA 6
SANSKRIT:
यस्तु सर्वाणि भूतानि आत्मन्येवानुपश्यति ।
MEANING:
He who sees all beings in his own Self and his own Self in all beings feels no hatred.
TAGS:
self, unity, compassion
---

---
REF: ISHA 7
SANSKRIT:
यस्मिन्न्सर्वाणि भूतानि आत्मैवाभूद्विजानतः ।
MEANING:
When a person realizes that all beings are one with the Self, what delusion or sorrow can exist?
TAGS:
knowledge, liberation, self
---

---
REF: ISHA 15
SANSKRIT:
हिरण्मयेन पात्रेण सत्यस्यापिहितं मुखम् ।
MEANING:
The face of Truth is hidden by a golden disc; remove it so that I may see the truth.
TAGS:
truth, knowledge, liberation
---
---
REF: ISHA 3
SANSKRIT:
असुर्या नाम ते लोका अन्धेन तमसाऽऽवृताः ।
MEANING:
Those worlds are enveloped in darkness, where people live without understanding the Self.
TAGS:
ignorance, self, darkness
---

---
REF: ISHA 4
SANSKRIT:
अनेजदेकं मनसो जवीयो नैनद्देवा आप्नुवन् पूर्वमर्षत् ।
MEANING:
The Self is unmoving, yet faster than the mind. The senses cannot reach it, for it is ever ahead.
TAGS:
self, consciousness, knowledge
---

---
REF: ISHA 5
SANSKRIT:
तदेजति तन्नैजति तद्दूरे तद्वन्तिके ।
MEANING:
It moves and it moves not; it is far and it is near; it is within all and outside all.
TAGS:
brahman, paradox, reality
---

---
REF: ISHA 8
SANSKRIT:
स पर्यगाच्छुक्रमकायमव्रणमस्नाविरं शुद्धमपापविद्धम् ।
MEANING:
The Self is all-pervading, pure, bodiless, untouched by sin, and self-existent.
TAGS:
purity, self, liberation
---

---
REF: ISHA 9
SANSKRIT:
अन्धं तमः प्रविशन्ति येऽविद्यामुपासते ।
MEANING:
Those who follow ignorance enter darkness; those who worship knowledge alone enter even greater darkness.
TAGS:
knowledge, ignorance, balance
---

---
REF: ISHA 11
SANSKRIT:
विद्यां चाविद्यां च यस्तद्वेदोभयं सह ।
MEANING:
He who knows both knowledge and action together crosses death through action and attains immortality through knowledge.
TAGS:
knowledge, karma, liberation
