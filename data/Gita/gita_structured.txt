---
REF: BG 2.47
SANSKRIT:
कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन ।
MEANING:
You have a right to perform your prescribed duty, but you are not entitled to the fruits of action.
TAGS:
karma, duty, action, detachment
---

REF: BG 2.48
SANSKRIT:
योगस्थः कुरु कर्माणि सङ्गं त्यक्त्वा धनञ्जय ।
MEANING:
Perform your duty with steadiness of mind, abandoning attachment to success and failure.
TAGS:
detachment, balance, karma, yoga

MEANING:

duty, selfless_action, karma
