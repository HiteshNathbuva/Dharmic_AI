[RAMA-1.1]
Text: Valmiki asked Narada: “Who among men is full of virtues, brave, truthful, firm in vow, compassionate, learned, and pleasing in appearance?”

[RAMA-1.2]
Text: Narada replied: “There is a prince named Rama, born in the Ikshvaku line, who possesses all these qualities.”

[RAMA-2.18]
Text: When Rama was exiled, the people of Ayodhya lamented, saying, “A kingdom without Rama is like a forest.”

[RAMA-3.11]
Text: Ravana, filled with arrogance, abducted Sita, believing himself invincible.

[RAMA-6.128]
Text: Rama returned to Ayodhya and was crowned king. The people rejoiced, and righteousness flourished.
