[VP-1.1]
Text: Maitreya said to Parasara: “How did creation arise, and what is the nature of the Supreme Being?”

[VP-1.2]
Text: Parasara replied: “Vishnu alone is the cause of all causes. From Him this universe manifests, is sustained, and is dissolved.”

[VP-2.4]
Text: Time moves in cycles, and the universe follows the rhythms of creation and dissolution.
